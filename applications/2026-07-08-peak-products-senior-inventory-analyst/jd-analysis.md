> Extracted by Claude acting as senior technical recruiter.

## 1. Hard Requirements (knockouts)

- Bachelor's degree or college diploma in Supply Chain Management, Business, Engineering, Logistics, or a related field.
- 3+ years of experience in inventory planning, demand forecasting, or supply chain operations within a manufacturing or distribution environment.
- 3+ years performing historical sales analysis and developing demand forecasts for purchasing / replenishment / inventory planning.
- 3+ years creating, managing, and monitoring purchase orders and coordinating with suppliers or vendors.
- 3+ years working cross-functionally with production, sales, customer service, and logistics.
- Demonstrated proficiency with ERP systems (such as SAP or NetSuite) and advanced Excel.
