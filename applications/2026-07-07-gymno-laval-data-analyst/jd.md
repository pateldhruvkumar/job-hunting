## Full job description

### About the Role

We're looking for a detail-oriented and curious Data Analyst to join a growing team where your insights will directly influence real business decisions. If you enjoy turning raw data into clear stories, solving problems, and working with a supportive, fast-moving team, this role is for you.

This is a great opportunity for someone early in their data career or looking to grow into more advanced analytics, data science, or business intelligence roles.

### What You'll Do

- Analyze datasets to identify trends, patterns, and actionable insights
- Build and maintain dashboards and reports (Excel, SQL, Tableau/Power BI or similar tools)
- Work closely with teams like marketing, product, and operations to support decision-making
- Clean, validate, and organize data from multiple sources
- Present findings in a clear, easy-to-understand way (even for non-technical audiences)
- Support ongoing reporting and ad hoc analysis requests

### What We're Looking For

- Experience with data analysis tools (Excel required; SQL strongly preferred)
- Familiarity with BI tools (Tableau, Power BI, Looker, etc. is a plus)
- Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
- Ability to communicate insights clearly and simply
- Bachelor's degree in a related field is preferred but not required (equivalent experience welcome)
